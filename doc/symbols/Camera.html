Class Camera

A virtual camera that can be positioned free or relative to another object. The scene can contain many cameras and the real camera can be set to follow one of these.
Defined in: graphics.js.

Class Detail
Camera(aspect, fov, controllablePosition, controllableDirection, followedObject, followPositionMatrix, followOrientationMatrix, rotationCenterIsObject)
Creates a new camera object.
Parameters:
{number} aspect
The X/Y aspect ration of the screen of the camera.
{number} fov
The Field Of View of the camera in degrees.
{boolean} controllablePosition
Whether the position of the camera is changeable by the player.
{boolean} controllableDirection
Whether the direction of the camera is changeable by the player.
{VisualObject} followedObject
The object to which the camera position and direction has to be interpredet. If undefined, the camera position is interpreted as absolute (relative to scene center)
{Float32Array} followPositionMatrix
The translation matrix describing the relative position to the followed object.
{Float32Array} followOrientationMatrix
The rotation matrix describing the relative orientation to the followed object.
{boolean} rotationCenterIsObject
Whether the rotation of the camera has to be executed around the followed model.

Class EgomModel

Represents a 3D polygonal model. The Egom data stucture is optimized for generating and editing models from the program code.
Defined in: egom.js.

Method Detail
addSphere(x, y, z, radius, angles, color, luminosity, shininess, textureCoordinates, cullFace)
Adds vertices and triangles that form a sphere (UV sphere).
Parameters:
{number} x
The X coordinate of the center.
{number} y
The Y coordinate of the center.
{number} z
The Z coordinate of the center.
{number} radius
The radius of the sphere.
{number} angles
The number of angles one circle should have within the sphere.
{number[]} color
The RGBA components of the desired color of the triangles.
{number} luminosity
The luminosity of the triangles.
{number} shininess
The shininess exponent of the triangles.
{number[][]} textureCoordinates
The coordinate pairs of the texture to map to the sphere: from bottom left counter-clockwise
{boolean} cullFace
Whether to omit the triangles from the inside of the sphere.

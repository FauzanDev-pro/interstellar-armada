Help

Keyboard commands

Key Command
p Pause the game.
l Toggle rotation of directional light.
h Toggle the visibility of hitboxes.
c Follow next spacecraft.
x Follow previous spacecraft.
v Change view of currently followed spacecraft.
m Switch the control of the currently followed spacecraft to manual.
n Switch the control of the currently followed spacecraft to AI.
space Fire the weapons of the manually controlled spacecraft.
o Toggle the controlled spacecraft between free and compensated flight modes.
w Forward thrust (free mode) or increase speed (compensated mode).
s Reverse thrust (free mode) or decrease speed (compensated mode).
backspace Reset speed to zero (compensated mode).
arrows Turn around the controlled spacecraft or move the free camera.
page up/down Roll the controlled spacecrafts or move the free camera up/down.
ctrl + arrows Turn around the free camera.
z Zoom in. (decrease field of view of current camera)
u Zoom out. (increase field of view of current camera)
